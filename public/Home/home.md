## Technical Artist

### Real-time

I came from environment art and moved into Technical Art to work faster and produce better results. That background gives me a direct understanding of what artists need from a tool, which shapes how I approach the work.

My current role is building material frameworks and pipelines, developing in-house tooling, and overseeing asset creation workflows. I plan tasks and allocate team resources with project goals in mind, and act as a cross-discipline contact to keep deliverables on track. I train artists on engine implementation and studio pipelines, lead the team with a focus on individual growth, and look for ways to optimise workflows for large-scale production.
